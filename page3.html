Arabian Oryx Conservation

The Arabian Oryx

The Arabian Oryx is a species of antelope native to the Arabian Peninsula. Once extinct in the wild due to hunting and habitat loss, the Arabian Oryx was successfully reintroduced into the wild through conservation efforts. Today, it is considered a symbol of wildlife conservation success.
