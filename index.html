Welcome to reserves of Oman

Oman is home to several species of sea turtles, including the endangered Green Turtle, Hawksbill Turtle, and Loggerhead Turtle. These amazing creatures are part of Oman's rich marine biodiversity and play a crucial role in maintaining the health of the ocean ecosystem.

In Oman, conservation efforts are being made to protect these turtles and their nesting sites. Oman is one of the few places in the world where turtles come ashore to lay their eggs, providing a unique opportunity to observe and study them in their natural habitat.
