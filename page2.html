The Importance of Turtles

Why Turtles Are Important

Turtles play a crucial role in marine ecosystems by helping to maintain healthy seagrass beds and coral reefs. They also help control jellyfish populations and contribute to the balance of the marine food chain.

Types of Turtles

There are many species of turtles found around the world, including the Green Turtle, Hawksbill Turtle, Loggerhead Turtle, and Leatherback Turtle. Each species has unique characteristics and plays a vital role in their respective ecosystems.
